About us

I am Tubal Biswas the ex Church Leader of Choto Boyra Baptist Church since 2010 to 2018. Through Leadership I realized that Moral Education should be given to the unreached as the Bible says at Mathew 28: 19, 20. Go to the outer world, teach them as I have done and baptized. I noticed that it should be done quickly. Many of us have been waiting to get God lessons the real way of Life. So, I got out and started it to the unreached. Especially I chose a location where about 3 million people are under privileged and need to support the Spiritual lesson.

So, I made a plan to organize a team to teach them at every week. The Location is outside Khulna Town. It is about 30 kilometers away from The Town. Already there have been spread out the God Lessons. The devoted persons who have been jointly working on behalf of the Christ for the underprivileged are Mr Willaim Sashim Roy, John Samar Shaha, Robindranath Roy, Omela Mondal, and Stephen Uzzal Malaker who is the prayer nursing facilitator & adviser the neighborhood friend.
